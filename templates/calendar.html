This lays the course outline across the school year. Each unit spans a number of school weeks, and its lessons fill the school days in order, flowing around holidays and exam days. A unit with no week count is auto-sized to hold its lessons (at least one week); set a unit to 0 weeks to drop it from the calendar.

Edit in place: click a unit title to rename it; type in a unit's weeks field to pin its span (blank = auto); click an empty day to add a lesson there. Drag a lesson by its handle to reorder it or move it to another unit, and drag a unit by its grip to reorder units — every change saves to the outline.

The school year, its holidays, and exam days come from the calendar bound in the outline's plan.md (the calendar: key).
